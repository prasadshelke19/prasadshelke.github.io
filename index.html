About Me

I am a dedicated researcher and PhD candidate in Geophysics at Victoria University of Wellington, New Zealand. My research focuses on understanding Hemispheric Asymmetry in Simulating Atmospheric Blocking and Heat Extremes Under a Warming Planet.

Research
